history

Pacific Rim Group was founded in early 2012 to change the dynamics of the real estate marketing industry. We realized that although there are many options and selections for graphic designers, marketers, project managers and advertising companies; there are not industry specialized marketers who understand the fundamentals of each industry.

We set out to revolutionize the industry, by bringing value and convenience back to our clients. We’ve been involved from start to finish in both large and small scale residential and commercial developments from community plans to boutique projects. As well, we’ve been involved in the marketing, branding, and promotion of many junior mining and resource companies.

We have experience with all steps of the process, including: project management, consulting, marketing packages, sales and after sales.

Beyond Creative— we interpret this in two ways.

1. Providing work that is more creative than just any creative agency. We look above and beyond to find a new concept that is customized to your project and speaks to its identity and brand.

2. Doing work that goes beyond the creative. We manage your projects to ensure that it is done right from every angle and ensure feasibility. This way, there are less headaches on our clients and they have the ease of knowing it is being done the right way.

process

1.Market Research

In our opinion, at least 80% of our time and resources should be spent in this stage. Understanding the target market is crucial in identifying a marketing strategy and marketing plan. Market research helps use budgets effectively and save money where possible. It answers questions for us like:

Who are we targeting?
What are the day to day trends of these people? What is lacking in the market place?
What is the optimal budget to target this market? Is there something to revolutionize or change given these trends?

2.Creative Strategy

In this stage we set out a plan based on our market research. The big factor for us is separating ourselves from the crowds, or otherwise, how can we be BEYOND CREATIVE.

We put together clear and concise marketing plans for our clients to make the decision making process easier.

3.Implementation

This stage is the most rewarding for us. We are just as excited as you are when it comes to bringing our strategy to life.

We ensure quality every step of the way and stand behind our work.

4.Follow up

This is one of the most important parts— stepping back and reviewing our work. If we haven’t reached our targets, then we are not satisfied.

We don’t just leave you after our contract, but make sure that each of our projects become a success.

Arris Holdings is a junior resource company with a mineral claim located 12km south east of the ocean port of Squamish. The website has been perfectly designed for an ideal user experience which makes finding your desired apartment an easy and enjoyable experience. From Craigslist ads, to application forms, to websites, to letterheads and business cards… we ensured the brand and identity was kept throughout the company.
